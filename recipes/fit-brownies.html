Fit Brownies

This is yet another bombtastic recipe to help you stay fit and healthy on a consistent basis when those sweet cravings kick in. Sometimes you just need something chocolate. I got you covered with these high protein chocolate brownies!

--Ingredients--

  • 67 grams or 1 cup Kodiak Protein-Packed Chocolate Fudge Brownie Power Cup
  • 18 grams Great Value Light Whipped Topping
  • Any other toppings you would like and can budget in your calories (like a chocolate drizzle!).

--Directions--

  1. First, grab that brownie cup and follow the package directions to cook that baby up!
  2. Second, add whipped cream on top once it has cooled slightly and then whatever other toppings you choose.
  3. Third, enjoy the chocolate deliciousness!

--Calories--

  1. Kodiak Protein-Packed Chocolate Fudge Brownie Power Cup - 290 calories
  2. Great Value Light Whipped Topping - 40 calories
  3. Total - 330 calories
